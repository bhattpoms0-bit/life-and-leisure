Need Direct Help?

Chat with our luxury travel concierge 24/7.

How long does the visa process usually take? expand_more
Processing times vary by destination and visa category. Typically, tourist visas take 10-15 working days. However, Luxe Voyage offers priority concierge services for most major destinations that can expedite this to 3-5 days in certain cases.
Do you guarantee visa approval? expand_more
While no service can guarantee approval as it is at the sole discretion of the respective embassy, our 98.7% success rate is built on meticulous document auditing. We flag potential issues before submission to minimize any chance of rejection.
Can I track my application status? expand_more
Yes. Every Luxe Voyage client is assigned a dedicated visa specialist and gains access to our digital portal. You will receive real-time SMS and email updates at every stage—from document verification to embassy filing and final approval.
What happens if my visa is rejected? expand_more
In the rare event of a rejection, our specialists will review the embassy's feedback with you. We provide a complimentary re-evaluation and assistance with filing an appeal or a new application if the rejection reason can be mitigated.
